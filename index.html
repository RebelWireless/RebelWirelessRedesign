Why Rebel

What you get with every plan

No gotchas, no hidden fees, no retention department runaround.

Unlimited Data

No caps, no throttling, no "fair usage" fine print. Stream, game, work — use as much as you want.

No Term Contract

Month-to-month. Stay because you love it, not because you're locked in. Cancel anytime — no fees.

Equipment Included

Router, ONT, and any needed gear — included at no extra cost. No $15/mo "rental fee" nonsense.

Rate Lock for Life

The price you sign up at is the price you pay — forever. No surprise hikes after a promo period expires.

Local Support M-F 9-5

Call, email, or message — a real Calgarian answers. No offshore call centre, no endless phone trees.

Free Installation

Professional install at no cost. WISP or fiber, we come to you, set it up, and make sure it's running.

Built on Ubiquiti

Enterprise-grade Ubiquiti gear — WISP via airMAX/ac and fiber via UFiber GPON/XGSPON. Managed through UISP.

Same Price for Business

Small business or home — pricing is identical. No separate "business plan" upcharge. You pay the same $45 for 500 Mbps either way.
