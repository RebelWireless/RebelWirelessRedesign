# Rebel Wireless — Brand Guide v1.0

---

## 1. Brand Identity

**Company:** Rebel Wireless  
**Tagline:** *Internet on Your Terms.*  
**Tagline (alt):** *Calgary's Independent Internet.*

**Brand Promise:** High-speed internet that's fast, fair, and flexible — without the big-three runaround.

**Brand Personality:**
- Independent — owned and operated in Calgary
- Straightforward — no hidden fees, no contracts, no fine print
- Technical — built on Ubiquiti gear, managed through UISP
- Approachable — actual humans answering calls and messages

**Voice & Tone:**
- Direct and honest (like talking to a neighbour who knows tech)
- No corporate speak — "unlimited data" not "fair usage policy"
- Proudly local — Calgary references, local timezone, local support
- Confident but not arrogant — "we built a better network" not "we're the best"

**Target Audience:**
- Calgary residents tired of Telus/Shaw/Rogers price hikes
- Downtown apartments and condos (initial WISP focus)
- Small businesses wanting business-grade without business pricing
- Anyone who values local support over call centres

| `--rebel-blue-hover` | `#2563eb` | Hover state for blue accent |

| `--rebel-light-text` | `#0f172a` | Light mode text |

### Usage Rules
- **Navy backgrounds** for hero, footer, and full-width sections
- **Surface cards** for pricing, features, and content blocks
- **Blue** for primary buttons, links, and interactive elements
- **Cyan** for speed/technical badges (e.g., "UP TO 1 GBPS")
- **Amber** sparingly — best-value callout, availability indicators
- White/light mode is optional; default to dark

## 4. Logo Concept

**Primary Mark:** Wordmark "REBEL WIRELESS" in bold Inter, with a subtle bolt/lightning icon integrated into the "R" or as a separate mark. On dark backgrounds: white text + blue bolt. On light backgrounds: navy text + blue bolt.

**Icon Mark:** A simple lightning bolt in Rebel Blue (`#3b82f6`) — works as an app icon, favicon, and social avatar.

**Minimum Size:** Wordmark never smaller than 120px wide. Icon mark never smaller than 24px.

---

## 5. Imagery & Iconography

- Use **Heroicons** or **Lucide** for UI icons — clean, consistent outline style
- Photography: Calgary cityscape, downtown skyline, actual Calgary locations
- Technical imagery: Ubiquiti dishes, fiber spools, network gear (on-brand)
- Avoid generic stock photos of "people on laptops in coffee shops"
- Avoid AI-generated fake imagery
